Zvi Rosen

I am a Postdoc in the Song Lab in the Department of Statistics at U.C. Berkeley. My research focuses on applying tools from algebraic geometry and combinatorics to problems in biology and statistics. Topics from algebra I like thinking about: algebraic matroids, structured systems of polynomials, and convex set arrangements. Topics from biology I like thinking about: population genetics, single-cell transcription, and neuroscience.
